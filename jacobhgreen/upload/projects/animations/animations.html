3D Supershapes

An animation of evolving supershapes.

Supershapes follow a formula that extends spheres, circles and superellipses to complex 3D shapes (credit: Paul Bourke). The result is plotted in 3D using WEBGL. This is my implementation in JavaScript of a similar project by Daniel Shiffman. (I think the lighting needs work!)
