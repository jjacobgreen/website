Orb Waves

An animation of sinusoidally changing cuboids.

My recreation of a gif by bees & bombs. Daniel Shiffman's video of his version of this gif was one of his first videos that I watched, and it's been at the back of my mind ever since. Here's my pastel coloured implementation in JavaScript using WEBGL. (Sliders to adjust the rippling coming soon!)

3D Supershapes

An animation of evolving supershapes.

Supershapes follow a formula that extends spheres, circles and superellipses to complex 3D shapes (credit: Paul Bourke). The result is plotted in 3D using WEBGL. This is my implementation in JavaScript of a similar project by Daniel Shiffman. (I think the lighting needs work!)
