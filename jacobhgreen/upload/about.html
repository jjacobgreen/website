about

I graduated from the University of Oxford in July 2020 with a 1st Class degree in Engineering Science. In my first two years I studied general Engineering, covering topics from structures to microelectronics, which provided me with a strong theoretical background across all Engineering disciplines.

In my final year, I undertook a Master's project at the Oxford Robotics Institute as part of the Soft Robotics Laboratory. We used an industry-standard robotic arm fitted with a robotic skin to identify geometrically similar but materially different objects. More information about this can be found here.

My project, together with my courses, has given me a strong background and keen interest in both robotics and machine learning that I hope to take forward into my future career.

More recently, I have been honing my coding skills in Python and JavaScript as well as learning HTML/CSS. These small projects have mainly focused on short animations and games. Some of them can be found on the projects page.

All the pixel art on this site has been designed by me.
